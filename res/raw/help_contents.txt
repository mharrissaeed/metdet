<p1>All the reading displayed as a result are in "Micro Telsa (ut)".
<br>
For converting the reading from MicroTesla (uT) to Gauss (G). Use the following Conversion.
<br>
1 Gauss = 100 MicroTesla.
<br>
When the application is behaving abnormally. The sensors are not working correctly, sensor calibration is requires. For calibrating the device sensors.Rotate the device around the axis or in the figure 8 shape.<p1>
